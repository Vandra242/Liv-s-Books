Mission Statement

Liv's Books is an online library created by me, Livandra Wilson. This website is here to provide assistance to people in the world. I want everyone to be able to access a library. It allows people to come on and borrow any book they need, free of charge. Liv's Books wants to make sure that everyone in every community have a library so they're not disadvantaged.

We have fiction, non-ficion, adult, child, and teen.
